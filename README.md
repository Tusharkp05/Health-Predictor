_Health Risk Predictor is a user-friendly web application designed to evaluate your daily habits and provide personalized health risk assessments along with actionable wellness tips. By analyzing your meals, screen time, and sleep patterns, the tool helps you understand potential health risks and guides you toward healthier choices._

**Features:-**
- Meal analysis to identify healthy and unhealthy dietary patterns
- Screen time evaluation to assess phone usage impact
- Automatic sleep duration calculation based on input times
- Overall health risk classification: Low, Medium, or High
- Personalized health tips based on your lifestyle inputs
- Responsive design optimized for desktop and mobile devices

**Technologies Used**
- HTML5 and CSS3 for structure and styling
- Vanilla JavaScript for logic and interactivity
- Responsive layout with modern UI components
